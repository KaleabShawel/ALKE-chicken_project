Our History

For many years, ALKE has been Cooked the most delicious chickens at her home. In 2013, we opened our first store in Bishoftu (Ethiopia) in Main street near the Bus Station. People from our City fell in love with the chicken roast, Fried chicken, chicken burger... The house became a success in no time.
In 2015, no less than 100 Dishes were sold every day and ALKE Chicken moved into a larger building. After receiving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order Foods from the comfort of your own home and from anywhere in the Ethiopia. You are now looking at the result of this: our very own webstore, making it possible to order ALKE delicious Chicken whenever you feel like it!

ALKE is famous for using only the best ingredients. This way, we can make sure that our Foods are absolutely the best.
